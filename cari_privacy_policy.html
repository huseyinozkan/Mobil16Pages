PRIVACY POLICY

Version: June 17, 2025

We value the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data collected through our mobile application (“Application”) is collected, used, stored, and protected.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy covers the following:

  • Types of personal data collected
  • Purpose and legal basis for data collection
  • Data storage locations
  • Data sharing with third parties
  • User rights
  • Security measures
  • Changes to this policy
  • Contact information

2. Personal Data Collected

A. During Registration

  • First and Last Name
  • Phone Number (for verification purposes)

B. During Application Use

  • Records such as wallet, shopping, and vacation
  • Transactions added to these records: buy/sell, income/expense, collection/payment
  • In-app interaction data (e.g. screen viewing time, action timestamps)
  • IP address and device information (automatically collected)

C. Other

  • Voluntarily uploaded content (e.g. descriptions)
  • Notification permissions (if applicable)

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • To verify user identity
  • To prevent invalid or fake registrations
  • To allow the creation of records such as customers, wallets, and vacations
  • To manage user transaction history (e.g. income/expense tracking)
  • To improve user experience
  • Statistical analysis and reporting
  • To provide user support

4. Data Storage Locations

Personal data may be stored in secure data centers located in Turkey or abroad. These systems are protected with strong security protocols.

5. Security Measures

  • Servers are encrypted with SSL
  • Sensitive data is stored in encrypted form
  • Firewalls and authentication mechanisms are applied to prevent unauthorized access

6. Sharing with Third Parties

  • Your personal data is not sold or rented
  • Data may be shared, only to the necessary extent, with essential technology partners (e.g. phone verification, analytics services)
  • Data may be shared with relevant authorities in case of legal obligations (e.g. court orders, official requests)

7. User Rights

Under the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) and relevant legislation, users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of their information
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Object to data processing activities

To submit these requests, you can contact us via the in-app support or email. Additional information may be requested to verify your identity.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be announced through the Application. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
